Classic American Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Calories: 250/serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, egg, and milk.
  2. Heat pan, add butter.
  3. Pour batter, flip until golden.
  4. Serve with syrup.
